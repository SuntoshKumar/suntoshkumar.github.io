Privacy Policy for
Ashtanga Yoga And Meditation

📅 Effective Date: May 1, 2026 🔄 Last Updated: May 1, 2026 ✅ No personal data collected 🔒 All storage is local-only Health & Fitness · Android

1. Introduction

Sam Krish ("Developer", "we", "us", or "our") built the Ashtanga Yoga And Meditation application (the "App") as a free, ad-free Android application. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App handles, how it is used, and your rights with respect to that information.

By downloading or using the App you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please uninstall the App.

🛡️ Core commitment: Ashtanga Yoga And Meditation does not collect, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information. All data the App generates is stored exclusively on your device and is fully under your control.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. Specifically:

  • No account registration is required.
  • No name, email address, phone number, or any identifier is requested or collected.
  • No location data is accessed.
  • No device identifiers (advertising ID, IMEI, etc.) are read or transmitted.
  • No analytics SDK, crash-reporting SDK, or advertising SDK (e.g., Firebase, AdMob, Crashlytics, Mixpanel) is integrated.

Data stored locally on your device only:

  • Pranayama session history (technique name, duration, date)
  • Daily goal settings and streak count
  • Favorite techniques
  • Journal / mood log entries (text and mood rating you enter)
  • Reminder preferences (days, time)

This data is stored using Android's Room database and DataStore Preferences — both are on-device storage APIs with no network connection. The data never leaves your device unless you explicitly back it up through Android's system backup.

3. How We Use Information

All locally stored data is used solely to provide the App's features:

  • Session history — to display your past pranayama sessions and progress.
  • Daily goals & streaks — to motivate consistent practice.
  • Favorites — to surface your preferred techniques quickly.
  • Journal / mood logs — to let you reflect on how you feel after sessions.
  • Reminder preferences — to schedule local notifications (see §6).

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the App on your device.

4. Third-Party Services

The App embeds the Android YouTube Player library (PierFrancescoSoffritti/android-youtube-player) to display optional demonstration videos within the App.

When you watch a YouTube video inside the App, YouTube (operated by Google LLC) may collect information about that interaction in accordance with its own privacy policy. We have no control over and no access to any data collected by YouTube.

Relevant policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy — policies.google.com/privacy
  • YouTube Terms of Service — youtube.com/t/terms

Internet permission: The App requests the INTERNET permission solely for loading YouTube videos. No other network communication is performed by the App itself.

If you do not watch any embedded YouTube video, no network requests are made by the App.

5. Text-to-Speech (TTS)

The App uses the Android device's built-in Text-to-Speech (TTS) engine to provide Hindi-language audio guidance for pranayama sessions.

  • No audio is recorded by the App.
  • No microphone permission is requested or used.
  • TTS synthesis happens entirely on-device; no text or audio is transmitted to any server by the App.
  • The TTS engine itself is provided by your device manufacturer or Google (e.g., Google Text-to-Speech). If you use a third-party TTS engine installed on your device, that engine's own privacy policy applies.

6. Local Notifications & Reminders

The App lets you set daily meditation reminders. These reminders are implemented using Android WorkManager — a local task-scheduling API — and the standard Android notification system.

  • No push-notification server or third-party messaging service (e.g., Firebase Cloud Messaging) is used.
  • Reminder schedules are stored locally in DataStore.
  • You can disable notifications at any time via Android Settings → Apps → Pyrayanam → Notifications.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

All data is retained on your device as long as the App is installed. Because we operate no servers and collect no data, we have nothing to delete on our side.

You can permanently delete all App data in two ways:

  • Android Settings: Settings → Apps → Pyrayanam → Storage → Clear Data. This deletes the Room database, DataStore preferences, and any cached content.
  • Uninstall the App: Uninstalling the App removes all associated data from your device (unless Android cloud backup has copied it — manage this in your Android backup settings).

8. Children's Privacy

Pyrayanam is a general-wellness app suitable for all ages. It contains no violence, adult content, or in-app purchases.

Because the App does not collect any personal data from any user — including children — it complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google Play's Families Policy. We do not knowingly or unknowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the relevant age threshold in your jurisdiction).

If you are a parent or guardian and believe we have inadvertently collected information relating to a child, please contact us at the email below and we will promptly investigate and address your concern.

9. Security

Because no personal data is transmitted over the network by this App, the primary security boundary is your device itself. We rely on the Android operating system's built-in security model (sandboxing, app-level storage isolation) to protect locally stored data.

We recommend keeping your Android device updated and using a screen lock to prevent unauthorized access to your device.

10. Your Rights

Since no personal data is collected or processed by us, standard data-subject rights (e.g., GDPR right of access, rectification, erasure) are satisfied by the fact that all data remains entirely under your control on your own device. You may exercise full control at any time by clearing app data or uninstalling the App as described in §7.

Residents of California (CCPA), the European Economic Area (GDPR), or other jurisdictions with privacy legislation: because we collect no personal data, those regulations do not impose additional obligations on this App beyond what is stated here.
